## Governance Brief

Policy outcomes depend on execution capacity as much as legislation. This week’s governance watch focuses on implementation speed, institutional coordination, and the fiscal impact of pending decisions.

## What To Watch

- Parliamentary calendar and committee activity on economic and digital policy.
- Administrative timelines between federal direction and provincial implementation.
- Regulatory consistency for business compliance and investor predictability.
